ABOUT AVAIAA
Avaiaa was created from a love of travel, coastal living, and meaningful connections. It is a place where every traveler can find belonging, whether for a night or a season.

Avaiaa was created as more than just a place to stay—it's a hostel shaped by the spirit of Varkala. Here, travelers connect, share stories, and find a sense of belonging by the sea.
